# Cabinet Brand Guide — Carousel Edition

How Cabinet should look, sound, and feel across every carousel we publish.

---

## Positioning

**One-liner:** The AI-first knowledge base for founders who ship.

**Elevator pitch:** Cabinet is a self-hosted knowledge base where everything is files on disk. No database, no cloud lock-in. AI agents read your pages, edit your docs, and automate your ops — because your KB is their shared memory.

**Key differentiators to highlight in content:**
- File-based (no database)
- Self-hosted (your data, your machine)
- AI agents built-in (not bolted on)
- Embedded apps and terminals inside the KB
- Git-backed version history on everything

## Voice & Tone

| Attribute | Do | Don't |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| Direct | "Cabinet runs on files. That's it." | "Cabinet leverages a file-based paradigm..." |
| Builder energy | "We built this because Notion couldn't do it" | "Our solution addresses market gaps..." |
| Anti-corporate | "No sales calls. No enterprise pricing page." | "Contact us for a personalized demo" |
| Technical but clear | "Every page is a markdown file with YAML frontmatter" | "Our proprietary content management system" |
| Confident | "Your AI agents are useless without shared memory" | "We think AI agents might work better with context" |

### Platform-Specific Tone

**TikTok:** Maximum energy. Chaotic. Hook in the first word. Hot takes welcome. Think "dev Twitter but in carousel form."

**Instagram:** Clean and educational. Save-worthy. Structured breakdowns. Think "the carousel you screenshot and send to your cofounder."

**LinkedIn:** Founder perspective. Data when possible. Professional but not boring. Think "the post that gets reshared by VCs."

## Visual Identity

### Colors
| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Parchment | #FAF6F1 | Slide backgrounds |
| Dark Brown | #3B2F2F | Primary text |
| Medium Brown | #6B5B4F | Secondary text |
| Accent Brown | #8B5E3C | Highlights, CTAs, links |
| Terminal Dark | #2C2520 | Code/terminal slide backgrounds |
| Accent BG | #F5E6D3 | Tag backgrounds, subtle highlights |

### Typography Rules
- **Headlines:** Serif italic (editorial, sophisticated) — large, bold claims
- **Body text:** Sans-serif (Inter or system) — clean, readable
- **Labels/tags:** Monospace (JetBrains Mono) — technical credibility
- **Numbers/data:** Monospace — precision feel

### Slide Design Patterns
- **Hook slides:** Dark background (#2C2520), large serif italic text, one accent-colored keyword
- **Body slides:** Light parchment background, centered text, generous whitespace
- **Data slides:** Terminal chrome style — dark bg, monospace, green or gold accent numbers
- **CTA slides:** Accent background (#F5E6D3), clear action text, Cabinet logo

### Do's and Don'ts
- Do use screenshots of the actual Cabinet UI
- Do use terminal-style formatting for technical content
- Do use dot grid patterns as subtle backgrounds
- Don't use stock photos
- Don't use gradients that aren't in the palette
- Don't use more than 2 fonts per carousel
