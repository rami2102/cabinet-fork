## Script Templates

### Hot Take (5 slides — TikTok)

**Slide 1 (Hook):** Bold contrarian claim. One sentence. Accent color on the trigger word.

**Slide 2 (Why):** "Here's what nobody tells you..." — the reasoning behind the hot take.

**Slide 3 (Evidence):** A specific example, screenshot, or data point that proves the claim.

**Slide 4 (Reframe):** "The real problem is..." — shift perspective to Cabinet's solution.

**Slide 5 (CTA):** "Link in bio" or "Follow for more founder tools" — clear next step.

---

### Educational (8 slides — Instagram)

**Slide 1 (Hook):** Question or bold statement that promises value. "Save this."

**Slide 2 (Problem):** What's broken with current tools. Relatable pain.

**Slide 3-6 (Steps):** One step per slide. Short heading + 1-2 sentences. Show, don't tell.

**Slide 7 (Proof):** Screenshot of Cabinet doing the thing, or a before/after.

**Slide 8 (CTA):** Summary + "Follow @cabinet for more" + "Link in bio to try it"

---

### Comparison (6 slides — LinkedIn)

**Slide 1 (Hook):** "X vs Y — honest comparison from someone who used both"

**Slide 2-4 (Features):** Side-by-side comparison cards. One feature per slide. Fair but highlight Cabinet wins.

**Slide 5 (Verdict):** "If you need X, use Y. If you need Z, use Cabinet." — nuanced take builds credibility.

**Slide 6 (CTA):** "What tool are you using? Comment below." — engagement driver.

## Copy Rules

1. **Max 25 words per slide** — if you can't say it in 25 words, split it into two slides
2. **One idea per slide** — never stack concepts
3. **Write for scanning** — people swipe fast, they don't read paragraphs
4. **Use pattern interrupts** — "But here's the thing...", "Plot twist:", "Here's what nobody tells you"
5. **End every carousel with a CTA** — follow, link in bio, comment, save
6. **Lead with the hook** — if slide 1 doesn't stop the scroll, nothing else matters
