Voice & Tone

Do
Direct. Builder energy. Anti-corporate. Show don't tell. Write like you're explaining to a smart friend over coffee.
Don't
No buzzwords. No "leverage synergies." No enterprise fluff. No passive voice. No disclaimers before the point.
Do
"Your docs are files. Move them with cp. Version them with git. Own them forever."
Don't
"Our cutting-edge solution empowers teams to leverage AI-driven knowledge management at scale."
