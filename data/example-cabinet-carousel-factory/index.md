## Brand Guide

**Positioning:** Cabinet is the AI-first knowledge base for founders who ship. No cloud lock-in, no database, everything is files on disk.

**Voice:** Direct. Builder energy. Anti-corporate. "Show don't tell."

## Carousel Templates

### Educational (8-10 slides)

1.  **Hook slide** — Bold claim or question
    
2.  **Problem slide** — What's broken today
    
3.  **Slides 3-7** — Step-by-step solution
    
4.  **Proof slide** — Screenshot or data
    
5.  **Summary slide** — Key takeaways
    
6.  **CTA slide** — "Link in bio" or "Follow for more"
    

### Comparison (6 slides)

1.  **Hook** — "X vs Y"
    
2.  **Slides 2-4** — Side-by-side feature comparisons
    
3.  **Winner slide** — The verdict
    
4.  **CTA slide**
    

### Hot Take (5 slides)

1.  **Hook** — Controversial statement
    
2.  **Why slide** — The reasoning
    
3.  **Evidence slide** — Data or example
    
4.  **Reframe slide** — The real point
    
5.  **CTA slide**
